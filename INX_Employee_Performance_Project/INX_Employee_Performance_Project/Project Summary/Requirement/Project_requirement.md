# Project Requirement Document

## Organization Overview
INX Future Inc is a global data analytics and automation solutions provider with
over 15 years of industry presence. The organization is known for employee-friendly
policies and has been consistently rated among the top employers.

## Business Problem
In recent years, INX Future Inc observed a decline in employee performance indices,
leading to an 8% reduction in client satisfaction levels. Senior management seeks to
understand the underlying causes of this decline without negatively impacting employee
morale or the companyâ€™s employer brand.

## Business Objectives
The objectives of this project are:
1. To analyze employee performance across different departments
2. To identify key factors influencing employee performance
3. To develop a predictive model for employee performance rating
4. To provide data-driven recommendations for improving performance

## Constraints and Considerations
- Employee morale must not be negatively affected
- Penalization decisions must be objective and data-driven
- The solution must be interpretable and ethically sound

## Expected Outcomes
- Department-wise performance insights
- Identification of top performance drivers
- A trained machine learning model for performance prediction
- Actionable HR and management recommendations
